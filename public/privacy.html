Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024-09-14

GitLab AI Summarizer ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our principles regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our Chrome extension and related services (collectively, the "Service").

1. How we collect your information from Google

Following Google API Services User Data Policy

  • By Sign In With Google we collect your Google Id, Email, Profile Picture, First Name and Last Name to store in database and display in RanBOT website.
  • The scopes we use in Google Login:
    • .../auth/userinfo.email: View your email address
    • .../auth/userinfo.profile: See your personal info, including any personal info you've made publicly available
    • openid: Associate you with your personal info on Google

1. Information We Do Not Collect

GitLab AI Summarizer respects your privacy and is designed with this principle in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • Usage Data: We do not track or store information about your use of the Service, including your interactions with bookmarks, extensions, or tasks.
  • Device Information: We do not collect information about your device, such as IP address, browser type, or operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal or usage information, we do not use your information in any way. All features of GitLab AI Summarizer operate locally on your device, and any data related to bookmarks, extensions, downloads, and tasks is stored locally on your device.

3. Security

Although we do not collect any data, we prioritize the security of the information stored locally on your device. GitLab AI Summarizer employs industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

4. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any information, we also do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
